Thalassemia
User Researcher Information Architect Interaction Designer Visual Designer Project Manager
Overview

Thalassemia is a rare blood disease which can be identified using a simple blood test to find the values of MCV, RBC, HGB against the parameters of Age and Gender. Such blood test are not possible for people living in remote villages. So a preliminary screening test is done and results are determined using this application and further confirmations are done at places where such facilities are available. This application takes care of it all. Worked in this project right from the requirement gathering stage to development and it was not released because of the lack of funds.

To see the prototype https://marvelapp.com/j0ibeb

The Problem

In usual scenario a doctor who is specialised in Thalassemia have to go through the blood results and identify the suspected victims. Moreover you cannot take the doctor to all the remote places where people live and for whom the blood test (blood test is done doing a carriable blood testing machine which displays the various results) is done. Usually the blood reports are seen on the display and hand written on notebooks along with the tested patient identifiers and a digital report is made in some nearby city where facilities are available and sent to the expert doctor for opinion. After the doctor's opinion a resampling of blood of the suspected patients are collected and sent back to the research lab for accurate results.

The Challenge

The first and foremost challenge is budget. The person who brought the project to the company was an activist and he didn't have enough funds. For example I came up the idea of feeding the blood test results directly to the computer using a USB cable which came along with the testing machine. But unfortunately the cable was lost. Those cables are not available as stand alones but it comes as a package along with the testing machine. He didn't have money to buy a new machine.

Second challenge was the suggestion of a smartphone to input data and to develop an native app for the specific os. But he wanted to do something with the existing setup and not to invest more money in buying a smartphone for the project. he wanted it to happen as a second step and not immediately.

The Research

I wanted to see the Blood Testing Machine or the reports generated by it. But it was travelling along with the current team in deep Nilgiri mountain. So I had to depend on what the activist said about the machine and the people. Since this request was very much specific to this client I didnâ€™t bother to do competitor analysis. I met the doctor and another person who actually goes to field during these blood sampling trips. From the doctor I got the parameters that makes a person Thalassemic. From the worker I understood the cycle of work that they perform on and off the field while collecting blood samples.

Analysis

My main goal was to create a user experience that will be simple, effective and only provides informations that user would need for inputting the data and collect blood samples of suspected candidates. In the group of people visiting a village for doing the blood test only one will be computer literate and generally he will be the literate person as well. He will write down the candidate's name on the note as well as on a sticker that will be stuck on the blood sample. After collecting all samples in the village he will sit down and find the blood test results and note it don in his book against the name of the candidate. By this way the team will finish two three villages in one day. At night where they are resting this worker will input all the data into the application and find out the suspects. On their way return they will collect all the blood samples of the suspected candidates and send it to research laboratory for accurate results.
