About

I am an Architect by education, Filmmaker by passion & an Interaction Designer by profession. The varied domains that I dabbled with helped me to have greater understanding about life. I live somewhere where art and science meets. I am more inclined towards solving the problems of the world by giving an alternate, possible design solution. I am interested in walking the path rather than talking about the path.

Skills

Being an Architect I start any project with paper and pencil. I try to follow process as much as possible. I am emphathetic and always try to create parts with the whole in view. As for as the tools are concerned, I am good with Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, Axure and Final Cut Pro.
