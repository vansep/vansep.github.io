Groupsave
Information Architect Paper Prototype
Overview

Groupsave helps you set up a referral based offer which your customers can claim by sharing the Offer with friends on Facebook. I Created Home Page mockups, Information Architecture & Paper Prototype, for few flows and also Conceptualised a groupsave TV Advertisement. But none of it got implemented. through this project I learnt about how to do IA & do Paper Prototypes quickly.

To see the website http://www.groupsave.com

The Problem

The product targeted small and medium vendors like coffee shop owners etc. They are not so tech savvy and they didn’t enough trust on the product. They were afraid about their own capabilities in setting up the discount coupons for various products.

The Challenge

The challenge was to put all the required info within the landing page of the site. I gave two options one in which all the required info are kept above the fold and the other option in which there is only a hook info for a video to follow above the fold and other information are kept below accessible by scroll.

The Research

We did a small study with the shop owners and found out that if some of their friends are vouching then they will also join. And they had low self esteem in term of their technical capabilities. So getting the first few is the most difficult task and we decided to do it by removing the fear in their mind about technology/product by doing this add.
