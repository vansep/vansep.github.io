Consolidated Data Entry
Interaction Designer
Overview

There are many modules developed for streamlining the data entry operations done for an insurance brokerage and risk management company. Consolidated Data Entry is one of the modules out of 8 modules developed and designed by Pramati.Worked as an interaction designer in developing wireframes for the entire module.I learnt a lot about working within a restrained environment of templated design. Worked in collaboration with a team of business analyst. Learnt a lot about working as a team and not as an individual designer (Though design suffered heavily). I used Axure to develop the wireframes and also learnt the art of incremental design with available minimum information.

The Problem

There is not much of a problem to solve except the streamlining of the day to day data entry operations. Only this module needed a two way communication between the CSR and the candidate working in the Data Center.

The Challenge

Already 7 modules were designed and this is the eighth module. So most of the design elements were prefixed with the development of earlier modules. The challenge was to work with existing design elements and still satisfy the functional and experiential requirement in the best possible manner.

The Research

I went and saw the data center and its operation. I understood the functions of various roles within the center. They were mostly using keyboard than the mouse.

Analysis

It had very less scope in terms of design. SOlving functional problems was given more priority than aesthetics. Compromises were made according to client requirements. Over all I was not satisfied with what I designed. I gave them a proposal to reimagine all the UI elements from the existing modules so that it becomes more efficient. Proposal is under consideration.
