Logo Design (BIS)
End to End Design
Design Brief

To create a Logos under three categories 1) Logo for Bureau for Indian Standards 2) A family of logos for various Standard marks 3) A family of logos for various add on marks

My Approach

Logo for Bureau of Indian Standards should be identifiable not only in India but also in other countries and so it was designed considering regulating bodies of other countries and global context. Whereas standard and add on mark should primarily cater for India and should reflect India in its essence. What better symbol than swastik to represent a sense of quality. The design approach for these logos were inspired from Chinese hexagrams and MIT logo design. https://marvelapp.com/j16fie

My Contribution

I conceptualised and designed all the logos.

Learnings

Learnt the nuances of creating a family of similar but not same icons. Understood my inefficiency in converting the idea into final product (more to learn after the results are announced).
